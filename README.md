# India NewSpace Market Intelligence Dashboard
### Strategic analysis of private capital flow in India's space economy (2020–2026)

[**View Live Dashboard**](https://indian-newspace-market-intelligence-1.onrender.com/) | 
[**Download Dataset**](data/indian_space_startups.csv)

---

## Executive Summary

India's private space sector has grown from ~50 to 300+ startups since the 2020 liberalisation reforms. 
This dashboard maps the funding landscape across 40 companies to identify where capital is concentrated, 
where gaps exist relative to global benchmarks, and which segments represent the highest strategic opportunity.

**Three findings a strategy team needs to know:**
1. Launch vehicles capture 20.1% of Indian NewSpace funding vs 34% globally — highest concentration risk
2. SSA holds 6.8% of Indian investment vs 9% globally — the clearest white space in the ecosystem  
3. 35% of active startups were founded post-2020 — policy reform as the primary growth driver

---

## What This Dashboard Analyses

- **Funding Distribution:** How private capital flows across 6 segments
- **Segment Maturity:** Startup density vs funding density per segment  
- **India vs Global Benchmarks:** Side-by-side comparison with global private space investment mix (BryceTech 2024)
- **Founding Velocity:** Startup formation rate before and after 2020 policy reforms
- **Startup Profiles:** Business model and competitive analysis of 5 key companies

pursuing a role in the sector. The global benchmark comparison uses data from BryceTech's
